# Fitness Criteria: Viral Tweet

## Goal
Evolve the most engaging, shareable, viral tweet possible. The topic is announcing an AI developer tool — but the format and angle can change completely.

## Constraints
- Must be under 280 characters
- Must be a single tweet (no threads)
- Must relate to AI + building software in some way
- No hashtags (they reduce engagement on X/Twitter in 2025)
- No emojis in the first 5 words (looks like an ad)

## What "better" means (in priority order)
1. **Hook** — first line must stop the scroll. Bold claim, surprising stat, contrarian take, or visceral image
2. **Shareability** — people retweet things that make them look smart, funny, or ahead of the curve
3. **Emotional punch** — awe, humor, outrage, curiosity. Flat = death
4. **Authenticity** — must feel like a human wrote it, not a marketing team. Conversational > corporate
5. **Brevity** — shorter tweets outperform. If you can say it in fewer words, do it

## Scoring Guide
- 0-2: reads like a press release or LinkedIn post
- 3-4: has a point but forgettable, no reason to retweet
- 5-6: interesting angle, decent hook, but missing emotional punch
- 7-8: strong hook, feels authentic, would get likes, might get retweeted
- 9-10: screenshot-worthy. The kind of tweet people quote-tweet with "this." Would hit 1k+ RTs from a mid-size account
